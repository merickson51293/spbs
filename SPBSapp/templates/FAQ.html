Frequently Asked Question's

How Do I Order?

Click on ORDER button above and fill out the custom order form.
After recieving your inquiry, we will contact you with confirmation of order.

I filled out the form, now what?

After filling out the form, we will check our schedule, make sure we can fulfill the order.
We will then get back to you within one to two business days with confirmation and pricing.

How much notice do I need to give for ordering cookies?

We ask for at least two weeks notice for all custom orders. This gives us time to design and make the cookies exactly how you want them.
Orders made less than two weeks can be accomodated if there is still availability, however the more time the better.
